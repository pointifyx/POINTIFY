About Pointify

Our Mission

Pointify was founded with a simple mission: to provide affordable, powerful, and easy-to-use point of sale solutions for businesses of all sizes. We believe that every business deserves access to modern technology that helps them grow and succeed.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Pointify emerged from the need for reliable POS systems that work seamlessly both online and offline. Our team of developers and business experts came together to create a solution that addresses the real challenges faced by retailers, restaurants, and service providers.
